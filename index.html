The Manifesto

Three pillars. Zero compromises.

Each plank takes a stated ideal at its word and follows it all the way to its absurd, perfectly logical conclusion. That's the whole trick.

01

Economy सत्य-युग राजकोष नीति · Satya-Yuga Fiscal Policy

The Corporate Cleanse

No conglomerate may fund a campaign. The Arthashastra calls hoarding wealth while citizens go jobless a sign of Adharma — so electoral trusts freeze on day one. If the scripture's good enough to quote, it's good enough to obey.

The Paper-Leak Penalty

Any minister or bureaucrat presiding over a leaked board or recruitment exam is barred from office and assigned five years of unpaid manual labour — building the public schools they failed.

02

Governance राम-राज्य जवाबदेही · Ram-Rajya Accountability

The Defection Curse

Switch parties after an election and you are declared spiritually and politically dead — barred from contesting anything for 25 years. Abandon the voters' mandate, abandon your Dharma.

Symmetric Scrutiny

To prove the agencies are truly neutral, 75% of all active corruption probes must point at whoever currently holds the treasury benches. Power should be investigated hardest, not least.

03

Media & Society पूर्ण पारदर्शिता · Absolute Translucency

The Calm-Anchor Act

Anchors who shout, manufacture panic, or read the state's press notes aloud lose their primetime slot — replaced by quiet documentaries on science, philosophy, and history. Heat down, light up.

Membership
